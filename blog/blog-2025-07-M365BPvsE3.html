FAQs

Can I mix Business Premium and E3 licences?

Yes, you can assign different plans to different users based on need. Just make sure policies like DLP and Defender are scoped properly.

What happens if I exceed 300 users on Business Premium?

Microsoft will prevent adding new Business Premium users. You’ll need to migrate some to E3 or contact your partner for alternatives.

Does Business Premium include Teams and OneDrive?

Yes, both are included with 1TB OneDrive storage per user.
