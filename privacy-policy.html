Privacy Policy

How Titan Solutions collects, uses, stores, and safeguards personal and business information.

Last updated: 6 May 2026

Titan Solutions respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information collected through this website and while delivering advisory and consulting services.

This policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected in line with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Services are provided to businesses and adult professionals only.

Information We Collect

Titan Solutions may collect information including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company or business name
  • Appointment or booking details
  • Technical or operational information voluntarily shared during enquiries or engagements

How Information Is Collected

Information may be collected when you submit forms, book meetings using Microsoft Bookings, communicate via email or phone, interact with this website, or provide information during advisory discussions.

Certain technical information may also be collected automatically through analytics and diagnostic tools.

Website Analytics and Tracking

To understand site usage and improve visitor experience, we use analytics tools including Microsoft Clarity. These tools may collect data such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and session interactions.

How Information Is Used

Collected information may be used to respond to enquiries, arrange advisory sessions, deliver consulting services, maintain client records, and improve website and service delivery. Titan Solutions does not sell personal information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Information may be stored using trusted cloud platforms, including Microsoft and HubSpot systems.

While strong safeguards are used, no electronic system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Overseas Disclosure

Some service providers used by Titan Solutions may store or process information outside Australia through global cloud infrastructure.

Access and Correction

You may request access to personal information held by Titan Solutions or request corrections where required. We aim to respond to privacy-related enquiries within five business days.

privacy@titansolutions.com.au

Third-Party Services

This website and related operations use third-party services including Microsoft 365, Microsoft Bookings, HubSpot CRM, and Microsoft Clarity. These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in services, legal obligations, or technology. The latest version will always be published on this website.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or information handling practices, contact:

privacy@titansolutions.com.au
